Lagos Bistro Menu

Appetizers

  • Gizdodo

    A delicious mix of fried chicken gizzard, and ripe plantains stir-fried in spicy tomato and pepper sauce.

  • Plantain Chips

    Unripe (green) or slightly ripe plantains, thinly sliced, seasoned with salt and then typically fried or baked.

  • Spring Rolls and Samosas

    Stuffed with vegetables, chicken, seasoned with spices, and deep-fried until crispy.

Main Courses

  • Amala and Ewedu Soup

    Amala and Ewedu Soup served with gbegiri (bean soup) and a rich meat stew on the side.

  • Jollof Rice

    Rice cooked in a blend of tomatoes, red bell peppers, onions, and spices served with fried plantains, chicken, beef, or fish.

  • Pounded Yam and Egusi Soup

    Pounded Yam served with Egusi soup, a rich, thick soup made with ground melon seeds, leafy vegetables, and meat or fish.

Desserts

  • Chin Chin

    Crunchy, fried snack made with flour, sugar, milk, and a hint of nutmeg.

  • Fruit Salad

    Fresh mix of tropical fruits like mango, pineapple, papaya, watermelon, and coconut.

  • Puff-Puff

    Small, round deep-fried dough balls made from flour, sugar, and yeast.
